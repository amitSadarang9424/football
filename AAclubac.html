Here at Bundesliga Fanatic, we are taking an in depth look at the story
behind the most iconic trikots in German football. Last time around,
we covered the story behind the black and white Deutschland kits.
Germany’s second most beloved team, the FC Bayern München, is up this time.
Bayern’s uniform history is actually pretty chaotic and full of coincidences.
Bayern’s kits can even decide the outcome of
a Champions League and scare away the Devil, but more on that later.
The club also comprises a basketball, a hockey and a handball team supported
by the football part and wearing the same kit colours.
Besides, the club upholds a number of amateur teams
in women’s football, women’s basketball, baseball, field hockey, rugby,
cycling, volleyball, figure skating and others.
